On January 28 of 2023, GitHub reported having over 100 million users, making it the largest host of source code in the world. They also stated that they had supported more than 372 million repositories, at least 28 million of them being public repositories. With Microsoft as their parent company, they have 2,500 current employees and have made over $1 billion in revenue.
